Facilities

State of the art facilities are provided for your child while at school

Classroom
Lab
Library
Nutrition & Healthcare

Classrooms

Classrooms are well lit and temperature controlled, with an interactive board that supports teaching and learning methods.
An iPad or equivalent is provided to the children from classes VI and above.

The teacher student ratio is 1:25.Akshayah has the state-of-the-art facilities, including aesthetically designed staff rooms, well air-conditioned AV rooms, spacious classrooms, comfortable seating arrangements and playground.

Classrooms are equipped with E-Boards and Audio-Visual aids for 21st century learning teaching and learning. We ensure that our students have best learning experiences in classrooms and in the campus.

Labs

The school has good facilities for all the laboratories – Physics, Chemistry and Life Sciences. Students are regularly taken to visit other labs and industries.

Science Labs is a well-equipped centre with state-of-the-art laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Environmental Sciences. Many concepts that are taught in the classroom are reinforced in various ways at the science zone. From microscopes and anatomy models to various apparatuses and infographic charts, the Science Zone creates a stimulating environment for the curious minds to work on academic problems.

Due to the exponential development of the Information Technology field. It is a necessity nowadays that the students must know to operate a computer as early as possible. The school has a well resourced computer lab linked through a dedicated hi-speed internet connection.

Students work in a variety of ways, using word processing, databases, graphic presentations, and desktop publishing. The curriculum is also well-aligned to their needs for progressive learning of computer operation and the science behind it.

Library

The library is well equipped with Encylopaedia Britannica, reference and fiction/non-fiction books.

The library maintains a supportive and a highly nurturing environment in building knowledge bank for students. The library contains books, CD-ROMs, DVDs that cover a spectrum of subjects and satisfies the students’ urge to learn.

We highly encourage our students to spend the library hours effectively, where they sit quietly and immerse themselves in books under a pleasant atmosphere. Library also has resources for continuing professional development in teaching staff.

Nutrition & Healthcare

The school provides packaged drinking water to keep the child refreshed and hydrated through the day, a nutritious snack/drink/lunch is provided.

Ophthalmologists, Dentists and General physicians visit the school regularly for a complete checkup to ensure the child’s healthy growth.

Students are taken care when in need at the Sick Bay, which houses students who are unwell. The bay is always manned with a qualified and professional nurse who will be available on site during the school hours. The nurse treats minor injuries, provides first aid and maintains students health records.
