Vibhaker, Head – Operations of AkshayaH School brings with him a wealth of rich global experience across different industries. Prior to this he was Head-Operations at Hindustan International Schools.

Vibhaker’s impressive background includes work with the Healthcare Industry as the Chief Operating Officer of Meitra Hospital, Calicut, and Head – Non Medical Services at Narayana Hrudayalaya, a renowned health care chain with 19 hospitals all over India.

With the Television Media, he was Executive Director and member of the advisory board at Ariana Radio and Television, Afghanistan; Chief Operating Officer of Jai Broadcasting Private Limited; President Operations at Saimira Access Technologies; Sales Director with Shin Satellite Plc and the Chief Operating Officer of Indiavision Satellite Communications Ltd. that was ranked first among Malayalam news channels and awarded the AVT Grihalakshmi award. BBC World had interviewed him for his innovative initiative. As Vice President - Operations of Asianet Communications Private Limited., Vibhaker was one of the key-founding members of the first privately owned TV channel in India.

An avid sportsman having excelled in several disciplines of sports, he represented Tamilnadu in Cycling, captained Maharashtra state team in Karate, and also excelled in Football having played for the school and college teams. As a keen photographer, he has contributed to leading magazines. Vibhaker has lived, travelled and worked in 40 countries across the globe.
